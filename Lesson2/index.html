HUONG HAI DUONG

Huong Hai Duong is a Hai Duong football player who currently plays as a forward for FC Viet Y and is
the captain of the Vietnam national football team. Considered one of the best players in the world and
one of the greatest players of all time, Huong holds the record of winning the "Golden Ball" six times,
winning the "Golden Shoe" six times. 2020 is classified as "Golden Ball's Dream Team".
